The 8 Pulses

Each Pulse grants a passive ability and a devastating awakening. Choose wisely.

🐉 Dragon Pulse

Passive

Obtained by right-clicking the dragon egg. Shoots dragon's breath every 5 hits. Player gets Regen II around end crystals.

Awakening

Builds obsidian walls around the player. Anyone trapped inside takes 1 heart/sec from purple particles. Wind charges out, but you'll get slowed.

🌍 Earth Pulse

Passive

Triggers a creeper explosion every 10 hits dealing 3 hearts of damage.

Awakening

Shatters the ground in a wide radius for 5 hearts. Blocks erupt and fall — enemies hit take an extra 3 hearts.

💨 Speed Pulse

Passive

Dash 2-3 blocks forward like a speed infuse. Permanent Speed I.

Awakening

Summons a speed tornado of white particles dealing 1 heart/sec and pulling enemies in. Alt: Speed III + dash-through deals 3 hearts and knockback.

💪 Strength Pulse

Passive

Permanent Strength I. After 10 hits you can dual-wield swords with forced-crit combos.

Awakening

Three giant swords drop from the sky for 5 hearts each. Alt: TP to the Nether — you deal 2x damage, enemy gets Weakness and glows red.

🪶 Feather Pulse

Passive

No fall damage + double jump. Every 10 hits releases a thorns-like particle ring (1 heart + knockback).

Awakening

Soar into the sky — your weapon acts as a mace. Alt: Unleash a white particle ring that knocks enemies back for 3 hearts.

🔥 Fire Pulse

Passive

Infinite fire resistance. Shoots magma balls every 10 hits dealing 2 hearts.

Awakening

Lava walls surround the area. Massive magma balls deal 5 hearts — enemies can dodge if quick enough.

⚡ Thunder Pulse

Passive

Strikes lightning every 10 seconds for 1-2 hearts of damage.

Awakening

Lightning rains everywhere. Player gets Strength III for 10s. Enemies struck 3-4 times for 2 hearts each.

🌊 Ocean Pulse

Passive

Infinite water breathing. +1 damage underwater.

Awakening

Summons a tidal wave in 4 directions. Enemies hit take 4 hearts and massive knockback.
